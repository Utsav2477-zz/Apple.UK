* Terms and Conditions

0% for 12 or 24 month instalment are available for purchases over $500 SGD for a limited time only. Orders must be placed in the Apple Online Store (via Telesales, 1800-692-7753 or ) using a DBS or Citi credit card. Purchases from Apple Retail Stores can be made using an OCBC, Citi, DBS/POSB, UOB, HSBC or Standard Chartered credit card. Orders placed with the Online Store (via Telesales, 1800-692-7753 or Chat) and paid for using 0% instalments are not eligible for Apple Store Pick-up.


Product Inclusions

0% for 12 or 24 months instalment offers are available on all products where the purchase amount is over $500 including Mac, iPad, Watch and iPhone.


Conditions of 0% Instalments 12 or 24 Months

Instalment plans in Apple Retail Stores are available to qualified Singapore residents and are offered by DBS/POSB, OCBC, Citi, UOB, HSBC and Standard Chartered. All purchases on 0% instalments are subject to approval by your credit card issuer. If your purchase is not approved by your credit card issuer for the 0% instalments Apple staff will not be informed of the reason. For further information on the outcome of your instalment purchase please contact your credit card issuer. Refer to DBS/POSB, OCBC, Citi, UOB, HSBC or Standard Chartered websites for conditions, fees and charges. For Citi Bank instalment purchases, full disclaimers, terms and conditions apply to individual products and banking services of Citibank Singapore Limited Co. Reg. No. 200309485K. Purchases on AMEX and Debit Cards are not eligible for these offers. Offer available to consumer, business and education customers. Corporate credit cards do not qualify for these instalment offers. Free delivery for all orders. Purchases will be billed to your account when the merchandise is available and/or shipped to you. Offer available on advertised or ticketed price. Prices are inclusive of GST. Finance charges will appear on your credit card statement. Corporate Employee Purchase Programme (EPP) and Refurbished Product purchases are eligible for the above 0% interest credit card instalment offers. Apple Employee EPP orders are not eligible for 0% interest instalments until further notice (Employee EPP orders with OCBC, Citi, DBS/POSB, UOB, HSBC or Standard Chartered cards do not qualify).


This information is current as of 1 April 2020.
