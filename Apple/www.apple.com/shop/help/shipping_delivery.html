Shipping & Pickup

You can purchase your items online and pick them up at an Apple Store, or have them delivered directly to your door. Here you'll find everything you need to know about delivery, pickup, and managing your order.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping is free on all orders. Items in your order may have different shipping availability.

Free next-day shipping:

Usually available on any in-stock Mac, iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch, or Apple TV.

Free two-day shipping

Available on most in-stock items.

You can see which shipping method items in your bag qualify for. Free next-day and two-day shipping is not available on customized Mac computers, engraved products, and certain order types, including orders paid for with financing or by bank transfer. Cutoffs apply. See checkout for estimated delivery dates.

Apple uses the best carriers in the business to make sure your order gets to you on time. Please note that we do not deliver to P.O. box addresses.

Guaranteed shipping services

In some areas, you can also choose faster delivery options for an additional fee.

  • Courier Delivery from an Apple Store → Delivers on the same day, within two hours (available during business hours on select items, to certain zip codes)
  • Guaranteed Next-Day Shipping → Delivers in one business day

Delivery times vary according to your selected delivery address, availability of your items, and the time of day you place your order. A signature may be required for delivery.

Ships Time

The Ships time is an estimate of when the item will leave our warehouse. For example, if an item displays “Ships: 1 business day,” the item will leave our warehouse one business day after you place your order. While Apple works hard to ship every order as quickly as possible, items that are personalized, custom-configured, or more popular may have longer shipping times.

Occasionally, we will confirm shipping and delivery times, then learn we are unable to fulfill the order due to low product availability. In these rare cases, we will notify you by email of the new shipping and delivery times.

Shipping cost

The cost for shipping is calculated and displayed on the Checkout page before you complete your order. Courier Delivery from an Apple Store cost includes tip.

You can find delivery costs at checkout, in the Order Confirmation email, and online when you view your order on the Order Status page.

Puerto Rico

  • Average transit time is 2–5 days.
  • Carriers deliver to Culebra and Vieques twice per week.
  • Overnight shipping is available to some zip codes for an additional cost.

Apple Pickup

If this is your first time selecting an Apple Store, our product pages will display Pickup: Check availability. Click this link, enter the zip code or the city where you'd like to pick up the item, and click Search Stores to check availability.

If you’ve previously selected an Apple Store, our product pages will display your chosen location. You can click Check another store to check pickup availability at a different Apple Store.

Once you're ready to purchase, select Pick up at Checkout. If you would like to change the pickup location, select Change location, then Continue. Items marked Available Today will usually be ready for pickup within an hour. We’ll send you a pickup notification email when your order is ready to be picked up. In some instances, you may also be able to select a window of time to pick up your order at an Apple Store. This Check-In Window will ensure that your order is available shortly after you arrive. Just check in with a Specialist when you get to the store. If you’re unable to come in during this time window, you can pick up your order afterward, but wait times may vary.

You'll receive a notification email when your item is available to pick up. Please wait for this email before coming to the store.

If you would like someone else to pick up your items, select Someone else will pick up these items. To pick up your items in an Apple Store, you will need to bring the order number and a valid government-issued photo ID. If you have designated an alternative pickup contact, they will need to bring the order number and a valid government-issued photo ID.

What's Next?

Once your order is placed, we send information about your order by email. The email you receive depends on whether you chose to have the items delivered or picked up. If you have multiple items on your order, you may receive additional emails for each item.

Order Acknowledgement

The order acknowledgement email provides a summary of the items in your order. If you chose to have an item delivered, the order acknowledgement will display the delivery address and a delivery date. You can click the date to view your order details. For pickup items in your order, the email will display your selected Apple Store location. If you have designated someone else to pick up the item for you, the email will also display your pickup contact.

Shipment Notification

If you chose to have an item delivered, we'll send a shipment notification email when the item leaves our warehouse. This email includes the carrier name, tracking number, and a delivery date. You can click the tracking number to track your package using Apple's online Order Status or enter the tracking number on the carrier's website.

If you purchase an Apple Store Gift Card, iTunes Gift Card or Apple Music Gift Card by email, we'll send you an email notification once we send the gift card to the recipient.

Pickup Notification

Once your items are available at the Apple Store, we'll send a pickup notification email.

Pickup Reminder

We'll send a reminder email if your item is still waiting to be picked up.

Text Notifications

We send updates by text message, so you can stay updated on your delivery or pickup order. Just add your mobile number at checkout to receive these notifications. The number of messages you'll receive will vary depending on the orders you placed.

What to Expect

Once your order is shipped or available for pickup, we'll send you a text message. This will include your order number and a link to the tracking or pickup store information. We will also send you texts on the day of delivery or after your item has been delivered, depending on your selected delivery method, country and carrier. You may receive 1-4 text messages per item on your order.

Changing Text Message Preferences

You can edit your mobile phone number at any time by signing in to your account and editing the delivery or pickup notification preferences for your order.

Your privacy is important to Apple. So we've developed a Privacy Policy that covers how we collect, use, disclose, transfer, and store your information. Visit apple.com/privacy. You can also review our Terms of Use at apple.com/legal/terms/site.html.

Carrier Delivery Options

Apple chooses which carrier will deliver your shipment, and some of our shipments require a signature. Your Shipment Notification email will tell you whether a signature is required. The carrier may also require a signature at their discretion. The carrier may make subsequent delivery attempts before returning the shipment to Apple. Once your order has prepared for shipment or has shipped, we are unable to change the shipping address.

If you are unavailable to provide a signature, Apple offers an easy option for you to receive your package.

Pick up package at carrier's local facility

After the first delivery attempt, you can request to pick up your package at our carrier's local facility. Call the number on the carrier's Delivery Notice and inform them you'd like to pick up your package in person.

To pick up your package from the carrier's local facility, you may need:

  • The Delivery Notice
  • Government ID (driver's license, passport) that matches the package name and address
  • Second ID with your name on it (credit card, social security card)

The carrier can release the package only to the person included on the shipping address.

In addition to this option, our carriers may offer additional delivery options, which may include electronic signature or estimated delivery time windows, learn more at FedEx Delivery Manager and UPS MyChoice.

Shipping Policies

Shopping in the U.S.

Items purchased online from Apple ship within the continental US, Alaska, Hawaii and Puerto Rico. No shipments can be made to P.O. Box addresses, United States territories, or addresses outside of the United States. You may not export any products purchased online from Apple.

Sales to U.S. territories

We are happy to ship your order to a friend or family member residing within the 50 United States if your billing address is within one of the following United States territories:

  • American Samoa
  • Armed Forces Africa
  • Armed Forces Americas
  • Armed Forces Canada
  • Armed Forces Europe
  • Armed Forces Middle East
  • Armed Forces Pacific
  • Guam
  • Northern Mariana Islands
  • Palau
  • U.S. Virgin Islands

Simply shop apple.com with the payment method of your choice, and we'll ship the order to any address within the 50 United States.

We regret that we cannot ship orders placed in the U.S. to addresses outside the 50 states. Let us help you find an Apple reseller to assist you in purchasing Apple products locally.

Shopping outside the U.S.

There are more than 30 apple.com stores in countries around the globe. If you would like to purchase Apple products from outside the U.S., please see our International Store Directory to find out if a store serves your country.

Shopping for friends and family who reside outside of the U.S.

Would you like to buy a gift for a friend or family member residing in a different country? Visit the Gifting section of online Help for more information.

Sales to U.S. military personnel stationed abroad

If you would like to ship to an APO/FPO address, please see our page.

Apple's products may be found at your local PX/BX AAFES store. Please contact AAFES to find out if your base carries Apple products.

APO/FPO Shipments

NOTE: As of November 15, 2012, US Postal Service regulations allow the shipment of products containing lithium-based batteries to APO, FPO, or DPO locations. USPS still prohibits the shipment of stand-alone lithium-based batteries, such as a replacement MacBook Pro battery. If you intend to purchase a stand-alone lithium battery for shipment to an APO, FPO or DPO address, please contact your freight forwarding service before making your purchase to determine product eligibility and delivery options to your desired destination.

Apple does not ship directly to APO/FPO addresses. However, there are a number of third-party forwarding services that can conveniently redirect Apple Store shipments to APO/FPO addresses for a fee.

Here’s how it works:

  • Sign up with one of the freight forwarding services such as APO Box (Opens in a new window), or select another provider of your choosing.
  • Then simply return to the Apple Online Store for Government and Military Employees to complete your order using the new U.S. address provided by the forwarding service. It’s really that simple.
  • Apple will ship your order to your new U.S. shipping address and the forwarding service will forward your order to your APO/FPO address. (Fees and service vary by vendor.)

APO/FPO Shipping Charges:

Please note that in addition to the standard shipping charges billed by Apple Inc., the forwarding service has its own charge for getting your order to your APO/FPO address. These additional fees include handling fees, Priority Mail postage, and any optional insurance coverage. Both the postage and insurance are charged at published U.S. Postal Service rates.

APO/FPO Insurance (Highly Recommended):

For APO/FPO Shipments, Apple is not responsible for loss or damage to products during shipment from the third-party forwarding service to APO/FPO. Therefore, we highly recommend that you insure each package for up to its full value. Insurance costs range from $3.20 for $200.00 worth of coverage to $21.00 for $2000.00 worth of coverage.

APO/FPO Delivery Time:

Please note that APO/FPO deliveries can take up to 6-8 weeks depending on local conditions at destination. Forwarding services use US Postal Service (USPS) to deliver to APO/FPO addresses, and USPS package tracking is not available to final destination.

Please notify the Apple support agent that your return will initiate from an APO/FPO address. Please click here for the process.
