Veterans and Military Purchase Program

In honor of your service, take 10% off Apple products and accessories.

Complete the steps below to become eligible for special pricing every day:

  • Verify your veteran or active military status with ID.me.
  • Review and agree to the store’s terms and conditions.

Get started

Verify with ID.me*

Veterans and Military pricing is available to

  • Current and Veteran members of the US Military, National Guard and Reserve
  • Immediate family members who reside in the same household

Need more help? or call 1‑800‑MY‑APPLE.

* ID.me is an Apple service provider, and is only permitted to use your information for the purpose of verifying your eligibility for the Veterans and Military Purchase Plan.
†† We approximate your location from your internet IP address by matching it to a geographic region or from the location entered during your previous visit to Apple.
