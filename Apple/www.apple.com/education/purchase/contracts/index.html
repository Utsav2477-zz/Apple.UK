Education

Apple has the following state and regional purchase agreements in place, under which you may be eligible to purchase. Please select the appropriate purchase agreement for more information about eligibility rules and the ordering process.

Apple requires every customer and/or order to have a current, valid agreement in place. If your institution is not eligible under the listed agreements or you would like to purchase products not covered by the agreements, please contact Apple Inc directly at 1-800-800-2775 to speak to a representative.

If you have any questions regarding the information found under your State or would like more details about the Agreements available, please contact the Apple Contracts Team at contracts@apple.com
