Pay the way you want.

If you’re a member of one of the participating banks listed below, you can pay in convenient monthly credit card installments when you shop the Apple Online Store or Apple Retail Store. Minimum purchase AED 1,000.

How it works.

Shop and make your purchase.

Add what you want to buy to your bag. Complete your purchase. After you have made your purchase contact your bank to check eligibility. Terms and Conditions will apply.

Please note: Financing also available with Apple Retail Store purchases. Subject to bank eligibility, and timing for participation differs for each bank. Terms apply.

Prices are inclusive of VAT (5%) but exclusive of delivery charges unless otherwise indicated. The order form shows you the VAT payable on the products you select.

*Important: Free delivery on orders over AED 200. Installment financing is provided directly by participating banks only. After you make your purchase on the Apple Online Store or within an Apple Retail Store contact your bank to see what options may be available to you. Bank Terms and Conditions apply.
