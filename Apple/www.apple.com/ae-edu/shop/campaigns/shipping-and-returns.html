Shipping in a snap.

Fast, free delivery.

Get fast, free delivery on orders over AED 200 and on all iPod models when you shop the Apple Online Store.

Track your items 24/7.

Once your order ships, we’ll email a tracking number so you can track your order. You can also request text message updates on your order status.

Learn more about text messages

Returns made easy.

Return any product for any reason.

Returning a product is easy. Just sign in to Order Status or call us on 8000 444 0396, no matter what the reason.

Learn more about returns

Effortless returns.

Free returns: you won’t incur any fees, and we’ll even cover the postage. For eligible products, you have up to 14 calendar days from the time you receive your item(s) to initiate a return. Simply sign in to your account or call us at 8000 444 0396.

Learn more about free returns
