Help Me Choose

iPhone 11 or iPhone 11 Pro?

Here’s a quick look at the differences.

 
iPhone 11
iPhone 11 Pro

Camera

  • Ultra Wide
  • Wide

The dual-camera system in iPhone 11 shoots everything from 4K video to beautiful portraits. Use the Ultra Wide camera to zoom out for sweeping landscapes or vast interiors.

  • Ultra Wide
  • Wide
  • Telephoto

The triple-camera system in iPhone 11 Pro adds a Telephoto lens for pro-level portraits and the ability to zoom in on distant subjects like wildlife and sporting events.

Battery1

Watch up to 17 hours of video.

iPhone 11 gives you impressive all-day battery life and is fast-charge capable (fast-charge adapter sold separately).

Watch up to 18 hours of video.

iPhone 11 Pro represents a major leap forward — up to 4 more hours of battery life than iPhone Xs. Fast-charge adapter included.

Watch up to 20 hours of video.

iPhone 11 Pro Max delivers our most dramatic leap in battery life ever — up to 5 more hours of battery life than iPhone Xs Max. Fast-charge adapter included.

Display2

  • Liquid Retina HD
  • 6.1” LCD display

This advanced display stays bright all the way into the corners and delivers true-to-life color. So everything from photos to videos to games looks beautiful.

  • Super Retina XDR
  • 5.8” or 6.5” OLED display

This is our best iPhone display and the sharpest display Apple makes. It’s brighter in sunlight and perfect for HDR movies, with true blacks and more detail in bright areas like fire.

Water Resistance3

Up to 2 meters
for 30 minutes

iPhone 11 is water resistant to a depth of 2 meters for 30 minutes – just in case it falls into a neighbor’s pool. And it stands up to spills from coffee, tea, soda, and more.

Up to 4 meters
for 30 minutes

iPhone 11 Pro shrugs off all the same spills. But we double the water resistance to a depth of 4 meters for 30 minutes.

Materials and Finishes

Aluminum
and glass design

The toughest glass in a smartphone, front and back. Paired with strong, durable, aerospace‑grade aluminum.

Stainless steel
and glass design

The same tough glass as iPhone 11, with a premium, textured matte finish on the back. Paired with polished, surgical‑grade stainless steel.

Capacity4

  • 64GB
  • 128GB
  • 256GB

Up to 256GB of storage gives you plenty of space for your favorite apps, games, music, and movies. And room to capture and store years of photos and 4K videos.

  • 64GB
  • 256GB
  • 512GB

A pro iPhone deserves a massive amount of storage. Get the 512GB model to keep creating 4K videos and photos without worrying about space. Have fun out there.
