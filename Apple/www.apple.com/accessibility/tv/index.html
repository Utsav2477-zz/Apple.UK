Apple TV

  • Featured
  • Vision
  • Hearing
  • Mobility

Apple TV comes with assistive technologies that open up the future of television to everyone. Catch every word of a comedy with customizable closed captions. Explore the Apple TV app and the iTunes Store to find movies with audio descriptions. Or use the Siri Remote to navigate and search just by speaking.

Audio Descriptions Hear how the scene looks.

The Apple TV app and the iTunes Store are packed with movies that come with audio descriptions to describe the visual content of every scene. Just check for the AD icon under the title.

Explore all features for vision

Closed Captions Watch without missing a sound.

If you’re deaf or hard of hearing, you can use closed captioning on Apple TV to display the dialogue, music, and sound effects of what’s onscreen.

Explore all features for hearing

Switch Control Play games, movies, and TV shows. Without picking up the remote.

Switch Control, the powerful accessibility technology in macOS and iOS, is also available on Apple TV. It lets you navigate sequentially through onscreen items and perform specific actions using a variety of Bluetooth-enabled switch hardware.

Explore all features for mobility

Enable accessibility features on Apple TV.

  1. Open settings and select General.
  2. Select Accessibility.
  3. Select the features you want to use.
