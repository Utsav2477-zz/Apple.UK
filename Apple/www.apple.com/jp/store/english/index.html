FAQs

Does Mac speak my language?

Every new Mac comes with the latest operating system, OS X which is multi-lingual as standard. A simple setting change allows use in English and a large range of international languages. In addition, your Mac can be ordered with an English Language (U.S. style) keyboard, or Japanese - the choice is yours.

Why would I switch from a PC?

How much time have you spent troubleshooting your PC? Imagine a computer designed by people who hate to waste time as much as you do. Where all the hardware and software just works, and works well together...

https://www.apple.com/why-mac/

Are product manuals available in English?

Your Mac comes equipped with cables, manuals and other items appropriate for the country in which you purchased it, so these are supplied in Japanese. However, English manuals are freely available to download from our website - just click here.

What is the voltage of Mac/iPod?
